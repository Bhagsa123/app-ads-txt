# Privacy Policy — Videomate Player **Last updated:** November 29, 2025 Videomate (“**we**”, “**us**”, “**our**”) is committed to protecting user privacy while delivering an exceptional, cutting-edge video playback experience. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use and share it, and the choices you have. This policy is modelled on standard app privacy frameworks. ([Jobiba][1]) --- ## 1. Scope & Acceptance By installing, accessing or using the Videomate Player application (the **Service**), you accept the collection, use and disclosure of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service. --- ## 2. Definitions * **Personal Data** — any information that identifies or can reasonably identify you (e.g., name, email). * **Usage Data** — non-personal information automatically collected (e.g., device model, OS version, play counts). * **Service Provider** — third-party companies that support our Service (analytics, crash reporting, ads, cloud storage). * **Device** — any smartphone, tablet, or other device that runs the Service. --- ## 3. Data We Collect & Why ### A. Information You Provide * **Account info (optional):** Email, display name — to support account features, backups and sync. * **Support communications:** Emails or chat messages you send us to resolve issues. **Purpose:** Account management, support, and communication. ### B. Automatically Collected Usage Data * **Device & app telemetry:** Device model, OS version, unique device identifiers (advertising ID), IP address, app version, crash reports, playback metrics (files opened, playback time, buffering events), and feature usage. **Purpose:** Improve reliability, UX, analytics, and troubleshooting. ### C. Media & Storage Access (local) * **Media library access (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE):** Videomate requests permission to read your local video/audio files and thumbnails so it can index and play media. * **Optional camera & microphone access:** Only requested if you explicitly use features that capture video/audio (e.g., record or add clips). **Purpose:** Core playback, library indexing, optional recording features. ### D. Location (optional) * **Approximate location** — only if you enable location-based features (e.g., localized content suggestions). **Purpose:** Personalization and localized features. ### E. Advertising & Analytics * **Advertising identifiers & analytics events** for ad personalization and measuring ad performance (if you opt into ad personalization). **Purpose:** Monetization, ad targeting (where permitted), campaign measurement. --- ## 4. Legal Basis (where applicable) Where required by law, our legal basis for processing personal data includes your consent, performance of a contract (providing the Service), compliance with legal obligations, and our legitimate interests (improving the Service, fraud prevention). --- ## 5. How We Use Your Data We use collected information to: * Provide, operate and maintain the Service. * Improve and personalize features and recommendations. * Diagnose technical issues and fix bugs. * Deliver customer support and communicate updates. * Serve and measure ads (when enabled). * Comply with legal obligations and protect rights. --- ## 6. Sharing & Disclosure We may share your information with: * **Service Providers:** analytics, crash reporting, cloud storage, and ad networks (they act under contract and only process data for specified purposes). * **Affiliates or business partners:** in case of feature collaboration or integrations. * **Business transfers:** on merger, sale, or reorganization. * **Legal requests:** when required by law or to protect rights, safety, or property. We do **not** sell your personal data for direct monetary consideration. --- ## 7. Data Retention We retain Personal Data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described here, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Usage Data may be retained for analytics and product improvement for a limited, reasonable period (typically months to a few years, depending on the purpose). --- ## 8.
